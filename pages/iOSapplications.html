FieldBook

FieldBook lets you organize your work based on projects.

Each project contains your list of entries with numerous editable fields.

Use the built-in GPS and a 3rd party weather service to get accurate data for your location.

The in-app map places all of your entries where they were taken.

Say you use a lot of math equations in your field work. Store custom images from your camera roll in the app for quick reference later.

FieldBook features

  • Create custom projects
  • Create custom entries
  • Editable fields include:
    • Date, location, weather, sketch, photo, notes, sample number, data sheet, stop number, strike/dip, trend/plunge, outcrop description, structural data, and partners.
  • Export projects to Google Earth
  • Dropbox sync
  • Add custom resources
  • View entries on an in-app map
