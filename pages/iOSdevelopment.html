Introducing our first iOS app,

FieldBook

FieldBook is designed to help compile information while out in the field. FieldBook on your iOS device can easily be adapted to collect data for later analysis or to keep a record of your latest project.

With DropBox connectivity and data export features.

Projects

FieldBook lets you organize your work based on projects.

You can create projects with customizable entries.

Entries

Each project contains your list of entries with numerous editable fields.

Including: Notes, Location & Weather, Pictures, Sketches, Sample Numbers, Descriptions, Data Table, Strike / Dip...and more!

Mapping

The in-app map places all of your entries where they were taken.

Realtime visualization of your location data associated with each entry. GPS coordinates are recorded and can be exported to Google Earth.

Climate Data

Use the built-in GPS and a 3rd party weather service to get accurate data for your location.

Resources

The resource feature can be adapted to fit the needs of each user or project, Custom text and photo uploads can be used for quick reference of formulas when using FieldBook.

Designing for Accessibility
